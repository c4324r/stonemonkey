About Us

Stone Monkey is a Vegan Catering buisness based in San Diego. We make authentic vegan chinese food. In addition to offering an excelent catering service, we are also in various farmers markets throughout San Diego.

You can find us at:

  • Ocean Beach Farmer's Market on Wednesday from 4:00pm-8:00pm
  • Oceanside Sunset Market on Thursday from 5:00pm-9:00pm
  • Hillcrest Farmer's Market from 9:00am-2:00pm
